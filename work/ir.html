IRELAND WORK PERMIT

Work permits are available to Ireland in all the professions now. If you are a graduate and having minimum experience coupled with good English language, you are eligible for the Irish work permit. Your graduation can be in any subject. The total processing time of the visa is only one to three months after the successful visa interview by the employer.
Please fill our no obligation Assessment Form for further process.

IRELAND NURSING WORK PERMIT

The Ireland Nursing Program is a work visa program for nurses who are eligible to register and work as a nurse in Ireland. Qualified candidates will be able to work in Ireland and maybe eligible to apply for permanent residency after 2-5 years. The following are the requirements:
+ GNM or B Sc Nursing
+ 1 year experience (after registration)
+ 7 band IELTS (Academic module)

• Application process to NMBI

The Republic of Irelandis astate in Europe occupying about five-sixths of the island ofIreland. The capital is Dublin, located in the eastern part of the island. The state shares its only land border with Northern, one of the constituent countries of the United Kingdom. It is otherwise surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean, with the Celtic Sea to the south, Saint George's Channel to the south east, and the Irish Sea to the east. It is a unitary, parliamentary with an elected president serving as head of state.
